Ikoyi

Our history originated during the colonial era, the island was developed as a residential cantonment for the expatriate British community, as time went on many lands were reclaimed by it's citizens, Ikoyi now contains many other government buidings as well as businesses and houses the famous social club Ikoyi Club, and Ikoyi Golf Club.

Onikan Museum

The Onikan museum opened in 1957, situated at Ikoyi it has a collection of Nigerian art, including pieces of statuary, carvings also archaelogical and ethnographic exhibits. Discover more

Lekki Conservention Center

Discover the beauty of the Lekki conservation center, it is a 78-hectare Natural Resource Conservation, established in 1990 COME AND SEE! Discover more

Motor Boat Club

The Motor Boat Club is a members only boat club situated at Ikoyi It is recognized as one of the most prestigious club in Nigeria, founded in 23rd January, 1950. Discover More

Freedom Park

Discover freedom at the Lagos Freedom Park, it is a memorial and leisure park area in the middle of downtown Lagos. Designed by the architect Theo Lawson. The park was constucted to preserve the history and cultural heritage of Nigerians. Discover more

Nike Art Gallery

Visit the Nike Art Gallery at Ikoyi a gallery housing over eight thousand (8000) artworks from various Nigerian artist like Chief Josephine Oboh. Discover more
